Security

L-Trans places great emphasis on ensuring that that the goods we transport will arrive at their destination safely.


CMR insurance - This protects the cargo during delivery from reception of the goods to unloading.

Commodity insurance - this provides protection against complete or partial damage or loss/theft of the stock.

Drivers - When selecting our drivers, we focus on experience and reliability. Through continuous training and education, we ensure that our employees are well prepared to complete orders in all situations.

About

L-Trans entered the freight market in 2014. With our trustworthiness and expertise we have been the supplier of the largest chains of stores, we have been working with the largest companies in Hungary ever since.

We specialize in the delivery of foodstuffs and frozen goods specially designed for this purpose with our lift-mounted refrigerated trucks.

We believe that accurate, reliable service is the supreme value that we can provide to our partners.
